How to use KillerPDF, tool by tool.

Getting started

Download the single exe and run it, or use the built-in installer (no admin needed). Open a PDF by dragging it onto the window, clicking the drop zone, or pressing Ctrl+O.

Drop a folder or a .zip to bring in everything inside at once, choosing to merge it into one PDF or open each file in its own tab.

Annotation tools

What it does. Move, resize, and restyle any annotation, multi-select with Shift, and rubber-band select across pages. Double-click placed text or a stamp to re-edit it.

What it does. Add resizable, word-wrapping text boxes with an optional whiteout background fill, or edit existing PDF text inline with font matching.

What it does. Drag a box to lay a translucent highlight over part of a page, with its own color and opacity. For struck-through or underline-style marks, use the Line tool.

What it does. Draw freehand or drag straight lines, with adjustable color, opacity, and width.

What it does. Drop a PNG, JPG, or BMP onto a page as a resizable annotation, burned into the PDF on save.

Page tools

What it does. Trim the page with corner handles, on one page or the whole document, and remove the crop again later.

What it does. Stamp page numbers or a text watermark across a page range, with control over start value, format, position, and size, applied as a single undo.

What it does. Rotate in 90-degree steps or by a fine angle, scale, flip, and straighten a crooked scan by drawing a level line, all with a live preview. Annotations follow the transform.

Sign, OCR & forms

What it does. Draw and reuse signatures and initials, or sign with a cloud certificate (Certum SimplySign) and click-to-sign form fields.

What it does. OCR a whole page or a dragged region to the clipboard, make a scanned PDF searchable, or extract all text to a .txt or .md file. Extra languages download on demand.

What it does. Fill interactive PDF forms (text fields, checkboxes, radio buttons) as live controls and save them back into the file.

Navigate & find

What it does. Switch between single-page, continuous, two-page, and grid views, open several PDFs in tabs, and go full screen.

What it does. Full-text search with highlighting, drag-select to copy, the OUTLINES bookmark tree, and clickable links and cross-references.

Pages & files

What it does. Right-click the PAGES thumbnails to insert a blank page, rotate, reorder by dragging, extract, or delete, across multi-page selections.

What it does. Combine multiple PDFs, extract selected pages to a new file, and drop a folder or .zip to merge or open each item separately.

What it does. View and edit the PDF title, author, subject, keywords, and creator metadata, saved back into the file.

Output

What it does. A real in-app print preview with scale, position, margins, pages-per-sheet (N-up), color or black and white, and two-sided printing, rendered at 300 DPI.

What it does. Save a fully uneditable, rasterized copy with annotations burned in, or compress the current PDF into a .zip.

Reference

What it does. Six themes (Dark, Light, High Contrast, Blood, Greed, Cyanotic) with per-theme accent colors, a toolbar style picker, and a left-or-right resizable sidebar, all in Settings.

Help

F1 / Ctrl+?This shortcut list
F2About

Do I need an account or a license key? No. KillerPDF is free, GPLv3, with no account, no activation, and no subscription.

Does it phone home? No telemetry and no ads. Everything runs locally; the only network use is the optional, on-demand download of extra OCR language models.

Where is it installed? Per-user under %LOCALAPPDATA% with no admin rights, or run the exe portable from anywhere. It uninstalls cleanly from Add/Remove Programs.
